# **Verilog AI Tutor**

## What Is the Verilog AI Tutor?

The Verilog AI Tutor is a focused learning tool designed to help you **learn and practice Verilog HDL**. It is meant to support you as you write, read, and debug Verilog code during the program.

This tutor **only helps with Verilog-related topics**.

---

## What the Tutor Can Help With

You can use the Verilog AI Tutor to:
- Understand Verilog syntax and concepts
- Get help writing small Verilog modules
- Debug Verilog errors and unexpected behavior
- Learn how always blocks, FSMs, clocks, and resets work
- Understand simulation results and waveforms
- Ask questions about examples used in the program

---

## What the Tutor Will NOT Do

The Verilog AI Tutor:
- Will not help with non-Verilog topics
- Will not complete full assignments for you
- Will not replace learning how to think through a design
- Will not submit work on your behalf

It may ask you questions or suggest changes instead of giving full solutions.

## Using It Effectively

- Try writing your code first, then ask for help
- Use it to understand *why* something works or fails

Used correctly, the Verilog AI Tutor can help you become more confident and independent when working with hardware description languages.
